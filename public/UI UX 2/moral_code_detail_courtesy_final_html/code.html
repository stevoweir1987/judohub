Philosophy

In the Dojo, Rei or Courtesy is not merely a polite greeting; it is the physical expression of a spiritual attitude. It signifies respect for one's self, one's opponent, and the path itself. Without courtesy, the martial arts descend into mere violence.

True courtesy is born of strength, not weakness. It is the ability to maintain one's composure and dignity while extending grace to others, regardless of their status or the circumstances of the encounter.
