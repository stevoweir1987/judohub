Osoto Gari (Large Outer Reap)

Technical Breakdown & Drill Sequence

analytics Technique Breakdown

01

The Entry (Kuzushi)

Step deeply next to the opponent's lead foot while maintaining tight chest contact. Your lead shoulder should drive into their collarbone.

02

The Pull (Hikite & Tsurite)

Simultaneously pull the sleeve to your hip and drive the lapel hand upward and backward, breaking their balance to the rear corner.
