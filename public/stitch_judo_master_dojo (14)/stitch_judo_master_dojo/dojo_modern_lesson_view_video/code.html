Osoto Gari

Major Outer Reaping

south

1. The Entry (Tsukuri)

Step deep beside the opponent with your supporting leg. Ensure your foot is parallel to theirs, creating a strong pivot point for the reaping motion.

pan_tool

2. The Pull (Kuzushi)

Break their balance by pulling their sleeve arm across while driving your chest into theirs. Their weight should be fully on their right heel.

3. The Execution (Kake)

Swing your reaping leg high and sweep through behind their knee. Drive downward, following the opponent to the ground for a controlled landing.
